The Art of Remixing: Behind the Scenes of "Rainy day friction (Nicholas Burgess Mix)"

Subscribe to the show on: Spotify | Apple Podcasts | YouTube Podcasts | RSS

In this episode, I talk with musician Nicholas Burgess who shares his creative process behind the remix of our song "Rainy Day Friction," a track featured on the recently released EP "Lorenzo's Remixes Volume 1." 

Nicholas describes his approach to remixing as treating the project as if it were his own song. He begins by listening to the original stems, identifying key elements, and building upon them. His goal is to create a remix that not only pays homage to the original but also reflects his own musical style.

Nicholas explains how he selects and manipulates audio, uses MIDI synths, and layers the vocals. We talk about how he chose to focus on the chorus of "Rainy Day Friction," making it the centerpiece of his remix. Nicholas's remixing process also involves experimenting with various instruments and effects, creating a sound that is both contemporary and nostalgic, with influences from new wave and 60s orchestral music.

We also talk about the creative decision to add whispering vocals for a ghostly effect and balancing multiple instrument layers in the mix.

In this episode, I talk with Toucon (aka Connor Lipke) a producer and musician. He tells me the story behind the beat he created for the remix of our song "Slippery friction" on our EP Lorenzo's Remixes, Volume 1. 

He reveals how he uses tools like FL Studio and Serum VST to structure his beats with a MIDI keyboard and an electronic drum kit.

Toucon explains his process of layering synths and beats, often starting with a simple melody and building complexity from there. He shares insights into his workflow, including how he organizes and sells his beats through platforms like BeatStars.
